Tips and tricks for test-takers
BEFORE TEST DAY
Keep these tips in mind as you get ready for test day:

Check for testing policies on your exam program’s homepage


Review study guides and prep materials

IN-PERSON TESTING

Plan to arrive early to get settled ahead of your scheduled exam
Leave any test prep materials at home
ONLINE TESTING

Complete any necessary system checks
Make sure you have acceptable ID
ON TEST DAY
When test day arrives, make sure to:

Be well-rested and eat a meal or snack


Read all test instructions and rules thoroughly


Take your time and read the exam questions carefully

IN-PERSON TESTING

Give yourself enough time to get to the test center
Bring the proper IDs with you
ONLINE TESTING

Prepare your testing space
Have your acceptable ID handy
  


Prepare to pass
We partner with dozens of testing organizations, such as Microsoft, Cisco, and CompTIA, to provide industry-leading learning materials designed expressly to help you pass. Get your prep materials today.

Some test programs may offer exam prep materials directly, too. Visit your exam program’s homepage to find out more.

Be cautious of unauthorized resources found online. For the most accurate and effective exam preparation, we strongly recommend using only official study materials approved or produced by your exam program.
